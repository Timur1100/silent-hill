    • About the game

  • Silent Hill (Japanese: サイレントヒル, Sairento Hiru) is a 1999 survival horror video game that launched the video game series of the same name. Developed by Team Silent, a group within Konami Computer Entertainment Tokyo, it was published by Konami exclusively for the PlayStation. Silent Hill uses a third-person perspective, and all locations are rendered in 3D. To conceal graphical flaws caused by the PlayStation's technical limitations, the developers used a "fog effect" ( The game features five different endings, including one that's a joke).
